Report a Case

If you come across an animal in distress, please follow the steps below to report it to us.

  1. Step 1: Gather information about the case (location, type of animal, condition, etc.).
  2. Step 2: Take clear pictures or videos if possible.
  3. Step 3: Contact us via our helpline or email.
  4. Step 4: Fill out the online report form available on our website.
  5. Step 5: Stay connected for further updates or required assistance.
