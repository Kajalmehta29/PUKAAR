Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?
You can donate via UPI/ Bank transfer on our donation page.
Will I get a donation receipt?
Yes! After a successful donation, you will receive a digital receipt.
How do I adopt a pet?
Visit our "Meet Our Babies" section and fill out an adoption request.
How can I volunteer?
You can apply to volunteer through our "Get Involved" page.
What happens to the donated amount?
Your donated amount goes in paying medical bills,vet visit bills,food,cleaning supplies,etc.
How do I report an abandoned/injured pet?
You can directly call us and send the details through whatsapp number given.
Do you accept in-kind donations (food, toys, blankets)?
Yes we surely accept these kind of donations.
Can I visit the shelter before adopting?
Yes, you can visit the shelter any time you want. Just inform us prior so that we can schedule our timings accordingly to avoid any kind of inconvenience.
How can I sponsor a pet?
Yes you can sponsor a pet.
How can I spread awareness about Pukaar?
You can spread awarenessabout us by sharing our instagram account with your friends and telling them about us,our work.
