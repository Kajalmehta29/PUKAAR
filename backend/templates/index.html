About Us

Pukaar Animal Shelter provides care, medical attention, and shelter to stray and abandoned animals.

Pukaar is a Dehradun based small animal welfare trust started by 2 people. It is a registered animal welfare trust which works for the upliftment of the stray dogs and cats. We were established on 2022 since then we have rescued 300+dogs. Currently we have over 60 dogs and 3 cats under our care. We don't have an ambulance or a rescue van, we rescue on our two wheelers since many years. We do not have as such team all the work is solely done by two founders of pukaar. From sweeping to coking food to rescuing to vet everything is done by two people.

At pukaar we Rescue-Treat-Release and Rehabilitate if needed. But with the best treatment comes the best cost. We don't get any kind of fundings from the government and we totally rely on crowd funding. Please support our cause so that we can continue our work and give these innocent babies the life they deserve.
