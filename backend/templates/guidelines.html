Adoption Guidelines

We are thrilled that you are considering adopting a pet! To ensure a smooth adoption process, please read the guidelines below.

1. Eligibility Criteria

  • You must be at least 16 years old.
  • Have a stable source of income to support the pet.
  • Provide a pet-friendly environment.
  • No history of animal cruelty or neglect.

2. Adoption Process

  • Fill out the online adoption application.
  • We will review your application and reach out to you in a couple of days.
  • A home visit may be required for verification.
  • Upon approval, sign the adoption agreement.
  • Take your new pet home!

3. Required Documents

  • Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, Passport, etc.).
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, rental agreement, etc.).

4. Responsibilities After Adoption

  • Provide proper food, shelter, and medical care.
  • Ensure regular vaccinations and vet check-ups.
  • Show love, patience, and care for your pet.
  • Never abandon or mistreat your pet.
